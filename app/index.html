The Challenge

  • The nodes in the graph represent zones in need of WIFI.
  • A hotspot can be placed at any node and will serve that node and all the nodes connected to it.

Add enough hotspots to provide service to all the zones. Determine the minimum number of hotspots needed.

Instructions

  • Click a node to make it a hotspot. Click it again to undo it.
  • Only nodes that do not already have service can become hotspots.
